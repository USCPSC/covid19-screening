Individuals must answer the questions and then certify their answers are true and correct in CPSC’s COVID-19 screening tool.

If you answer “yes” to any of the questions, you will be denied entry to CPSC facilities and you will be provided further instructions.

If you are a CPSC employee or contractor who is denied entry, you must speak with your immediate supervisor or contracting officer’s representative (COR) and/or management company about telework and/or leave options.

Federal employees who make a false statement on this form could be subject to an adverse personnel action, up to and including removal from their position. It is also a federal crime (18 U.S.C. § 1001) for anyone to provide false information on this form.
